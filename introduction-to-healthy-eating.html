What is Healthy Eating?

Healthy eating is about consuming a balanced diet that includes essential nutrients from different food groups. It helps maintain energy levels, boosts immunity, and promotes overall well-being.

Essential Nutrients

Your body needs six essential nutrients:

  • Carbohydrates: Provide energy (e.g., whole grains, fruits).
  • Proteins: Build and repair tissues (e.g., fish, beans, poultry).
  • Fats: Support cell function (e.g., nuts, olive oil, avocados).
  • Vitamins: Help in various body processes (e.g., vitamin C in citrus fruits).
  • Minerals: Important for bones, muscles, and nerves (e.g., calcium, iron).
  • Water: Essential for hydration and digestion.

Food Groups

Each food group provides different nutrients:

  • Fruits & Vegetables: Rich in vitamins and fiber.
  • Proteins: Found in meat, fish, beans, and nuts.
  • Grains: Whole grains are the best source of carbohydrates.
  • Dairy: Provides calcium and vitamin D.
  • Healthy Fats: Essential for brain function and energy.
