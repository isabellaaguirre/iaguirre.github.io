Eating Healthy on a Budget

Healthy eating doesn’t have to be expensive. With a little planning and smart shopping, you can create nutritious meals that are both affordable and delicious.

Budget-Friendly Meal Ideas

1. Oatmeal with Peanut Butter and Bananas

Ingredients: Oats, peanut butter, banana, cinnamon.

Instructions: Cook oats in water or milk, add peanut butter, sliced banana, and sprinkle with cinnamon.

2. Rice and Beans

Ingredients: Brown rice, canned beans, onions, garlic, spices.

Instructions: Cook rice, sauté onions and garlic, mix in beans and spices. Serve together.

3. Scrambled Eggs with Vegetables

Ingredients: Eggs, spinach, tomatoes, bell peppers, cheese (optional).

Instructions: Scramble eggs, cook with chopped vegetables, and add cheese if desired.

4. Baked Sweet Potato with Black Beans

Ingredients: Sweet potatoes, black beans, cumin, lime juice.

Instructions: Bake sweet potatoes, top with black beans, season with cumin and lime juice.

5. Whole Wheat Pasta with Tomato Sauce

Ingredients: Whole wheat pasta, canned tomatoes, garlic, olive oil, basil.

Instructions: Cook pasta, make sauce by sautéing garlic with tomatoes, and mix together.

Tips for Affordable Cooking

  • Buy staple foods like rice, beans, and oats in bulk.
  • Use frozen or canned vegetables when fresh options are expensive.
  • Plan meals in advance and reduce food waste.
  • Look for sales and discounts at the grocery store.
  • Cook in batches and store leftovers for later meals.
